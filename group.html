Principal Investigator

I am an assistant professor in the department of mechanical engineering at IIT Madras. I earned my B.Tech from JNTU Hyderabad (2013), MS and PhD (dual degree) from IIT Madras (2019). I worked as a postdoc at ETH Zurich, Switzerland (2019-2023) and UC Berkeley, USA (2024) before joining IITM as a faculty.

My research domain is surface science and engineering at micro and nano scale. Typically, this involves interfacial interactions between solid, liquid, and vapor in any combination (except for vapor-vapor interactions). On a fundamental level, such interactions can be a matter of life and death for insects like water striders. Inspired by nature, methods to artificially manipulate such interactions by micro/nano solid surface texturing have remarkable effects on macro-scale phenomena impacting various fields such as energy production, health care, droplet computing, chemical synthesis, and so on. I am fully convinced that focussed research in this area can provide practical solutions to many challenging problems .
